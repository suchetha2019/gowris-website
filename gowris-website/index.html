Her mother vid, Smt Shamala Aswath being a Bharatanatyam Guru laid a strong Bharatanatyam foundation to Gowri. Gowri�s tutelage continued with her mother for many years. Gowri�s passion for dance took a deeper root when she started her training from Guru late Sri B.R. Thulasiram. Gowri is now the disciple Guru Sri Sathyanarayana Raju and hones her expertise under his guidance. Gowri is also trained in Kallaripayattu the Martial Art form of Kerala. She is a regular practitioner of Yoga. Gowri is also trained in Carnatic music and Nattuvanga.

Gowri has had the privilege of learning from veterans like Padmabhushan Yamini Krishnamurthy, Padmashri Vyjayanthimala Bali, Guru Smt Bragha Bessel, Guru Sri Madhava Rao. Gowri is a gold medallist in Masters Degree Bharatanatyam from Jain University. She is an A grade artist of Doordashan . She is confessed with the Aryabhata award for her contribution in dance for the year 2014.

Gowri holds Bachelors Degree in Computer Science Engineering. Though an Engineer she took the path of dance and made it her profession. Gowri is the dance director of Majula Gururajs Sadhana Music School and Sri Srikanteshwara Kalakendra. She trains many students aiming to maintain the tradition. She has choreographed many dance productions attaining high appreciation.

Gowri had the previlage of performing for many dance festivals like Vaarshikotsava in Bailur, Udupi District, Paryaya Utsava in Udupi District, Ramotsava in Kallur, Raichur & Shaktinagar Vaarshikotsava in Raichur District, Nrutyotsava Devagiri Temple, Bangalore, Manorama Memorial Performance � 2009� at World Culture, Bangalore, Ranna Utsava� at Mudhol, Sai Nrityotsava , Ankura Festival 2012, Kalabharathi Youth Festival � at Kerala, Ananya Nruthyollasa Bangalore and many more. Gowri has toured internationally giving performances at Dubai with the team Vidyouth. She had the privilege of performing with Padmashri Prathiba Prahalads Prasidha Foundation at Russia Bellarus and China.
